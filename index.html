What is Reflexion Health?

Reflexion Health is a medical software company that is trying to help solve the problem with reinjury in physical therapy. In the present, patients are coming into the physical therapists' offices once or twice a week for physical therapy. They are given exercises to do at home, but few actually do these exercises. Therefore, at the end of a patient's physical therapy sessions, the physical therapist believes they should be healthy on the belief that the patient has done all their exercises. However, because most patients don't do their exercises, they end up reinjuring themselves.

Reinjury in physical therapy costs over 100 billion dollars in costs, and Reflexion Health is attempting to solve that problem. Reflexion Health is creating a software application where the patient uses the Microsoft Kinect camera at home and do exercises assigned to them by the clinician. The clinician can then track their exercises at home and have a better understanding of how close to healthy the patient really is. The product is able to track the patient's movements and give tips and warnings so the patient can perform the exercises with confidence. The clinician is able to watch videos of the patients performing the exercises and make the final judgement on how far along the patient really is without the patient going into the office.

Corporate life was definitely different than the startup culture. There are definitely pros and cons to both. Some positives about corporate life is that because the company has already been successful and established, it has the funds to experiment with new projects. However, I can also see that moving up in a corporate company is much harder to do. Most have held the same position for many years and there is very little movement up and down the corporate ladder.

What I did at PlayStation

Sony owns many studios that create many PlayStation only games. Because these studios are owned by Sony, they do want the studios to do well. Therefore, Sony needs to create applications that these studios can use to track and analyze data about their games. This is why they implemented a web application that tracks first-party game data that employees of Sony studios can access and analyze data about their games. However, the data on this website consists of data from games created by all 50+ studios owned by Sony. Because of this, most of the data for a specific employee of a specific studio is useless to them.

My problem that I had to solve was allowing the studio employees to grab only data that they need. This is why I was to create an API for this existing web application. An employee of a studio is able to grab data for games created by that studio or a specific game. This allows first-party games to be able to programatically grab and post user data on their websites or upcoming maintenances for players to see. Other use cases for this API includes studios grabbing only their data from the database of all first-party game data and creating their own web application for their employees.
